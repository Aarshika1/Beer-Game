Beer distribution game

The beer distribution game (also known as the beer game) is a type of gamification that is used to experience typical coordination problems of a supply chain process. It reflects a role-play simulation where several participants play with each other. The game represents a supply chain with a non-coordinated process where problems arise due to lack of information sharing.This game outlines the importance of information sharing, supply chain management and collaboration throughout a supply chain process.
